Before You Give

Donation Rules & Regulations

RULE 01

Minimum Donation Amount

All donations must be a minimum of R20 (ZAR) or equivalent in other currencies. There is no upper limit. Donations below this threshold cannot be processed.

RULE 02

Tax-Deductibility (Section 18A)

HERD is a registered NPO. Donations qualify for Section 18A tax deduction under the South African Income Tax Act. A certificate will be emailed within 30 days of receipt.

RULE 03

Refund Policy

Donations are non-refundable once processed, except in cases of duplicate transactions or proven system error. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days.

RULE 04

Designated vs. General Funds

Donors may designate their gift to a specific program. Undesignated donations enter our general fund, allocated by the HERD board to areas of greatest need.

RULE 05

Anonymous Donations

We accept anonymous donations. However, to issue a tax certificate, we require your full name and email. Anonymous donors will not be publicly recognised without consent.

RULE 06

Prohibited Sources

HERD does not accept donations from sources involved in illegal activity, environmental harm, or human rights violations. We reserve the right to decline or return any donation.

RULE 07

Recurring Donations

Monthly donors may cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. Cancellation requests must be sent to finance@herdngo.org.za. No penalties apply.

RULE 08

Data Protection (POPIA)

All personal information is protected under POPIA. We will never sell or share your data with third parties. Your details are used solely for donation processing and reporting.

By proceeding with a donation, you confirm that you have read and agree to these regulations.
